GGHS Sui Wala – District Lodhran

GGHS Sui Wala — IT Lab Upgradation (District Lodhran)

The ITSM program brought a significant improvement to GGHS Sui Wala by setting up a modern IT lab. This initiative aimed to give rural students exposure to digital education and prepare them for a competitive academic future.

Inauguration: ITSM Project Lodhran
School Type: Government Girls High School
Budget: Rs. 920,000

Facilities & Work Completed

  • Installed 30 new computers with updated hardware
  • Provided UPS backup (5.5 KV) with batteries for stable power supply
  • Arranged new furniture and structured cabling for the lab
  • Minor civil renovation for better environment

Why this project matters

Students at GGHS Sui Wala now have access to reliable computer facilities. The project has improved their confidence in IT and created equal opportunities for female students of rural areas to compete in the digital era.

Impact: The upgraded lab gave a new digital learning environment at GGHS Sui Wala, allowing uninterrupted computer classes and helping students enhance their digital literacy skills.

Suggested next steps

  • Conduct digital literacy workshops for girls
  • Plan quarterly lab maintenance and upgrades
  • Expand lab with additional computers based on demand
