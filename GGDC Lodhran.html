GGDC Lodhran – District Lodhran

GGDC Lodhran — IT Lab Establishment (District Lodhran)

A landmark initiative under the ITSM program to empower young women of Lodhran by building a new, fully equipped IT laboratory with modern computers and reliable backup facilities.

Inauguration: ITSM Program (District Lodhran)
College Type: Government Girls Degree College
Budget: Rs. 1,100,000

Facilities & Work Completed

  • Set up a brand new IT lab with 45 modern computers
  • Installed new furniture and networking infrastructure
  • Added 1 UPS (5.5 KV) with 4 batteries for reliable backup
  • Minor civil works to create a safe, student-friendly environment

Why this project matters

This IT lab project opened new horizons for female students of Lodhran by providing them access to modern digital resources. It not only enhanced computer literacy but also created equal educational opportunities in the digital age.

Impact: The new IT lab at GGDC Lodhran transformed the academic environment, enabling students to conduct practical sessions smoothly, improve their digital skills, and prepare themselves for higher studies and professional opportunities.

Suggested next steps

  • Introduce specialized IT short courses for students
  • Conduct teacher training to integrate IT tools in classrooms
  • Plan for regular maintenance of computers and UPS systems
